Test the post-purchase customer email

A given period after each sale you do in your webshop we send your customer a custom branded email asking if he/she would like to add a review of the product they purchased. This is called the post-purchase email and is the most vital feature of the Lipscore software to increase the number of reviews. By sending this email we increase the chance of the customer actually writing a review by several hundred percent! To further increase the review frequency we also support the possibility of giving them a discount coupon for their next purchase (which again drives more sales), or a special message telling them that review authors compete in a monthly contest or something.
